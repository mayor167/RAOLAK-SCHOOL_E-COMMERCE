About our online store.

We are a thrifted-clothing store that sells clothes for both genders. We deal with all kinds of unisex wears such as roundnecks and polos, dresses, skirts, jumpsuits and bumshorts for ladies; as well as shirts, shorts and roundnecks for men. Our items are very neat and quite affordable. We've had different people coming to patronize all from different part of the country and we are planning on selling in wholesales so retailers can resell. Our mission is to deliver the best and quality wears to our esteemed customers both home and abroad, and to help those that are interested in this business to start their own too. By giving out useful tips and proper mentorship.
